FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTION (FAQ)

If I want to order, what is the minimum order quantity ?
Minimum Order Quantity (MOQ) is 1 ton
What about the packing ?
There are 2 choices of packing :
1. Box for Loose Container Loading (LCL) shipment
2. Pallet for Full Container Loading (FCL) shipment
Your mill is in Indonesia, very far from US. What about delivery ? Will it be a problem ?
Delivery to US are available every week. There should be no problem.
How long will it takes to deliver my order from your mill to US ?
Delivery lead time to US will take 30 to 45 days after product ready but that is not including production time
How long will it takes for production ?
Production lead time is 30 days after Purchase Order
Which grades are available ?
Our available stocks are 301, 304, 316, and 430. For these grades, we can begin production immediatelly after Purchase Order received. Other grades outside these grades have to be ordered before we can begin the production. All grades are available upon request.
What is your thickness and width?
We can produce stainless steel with thickness down to 0.05 mm and slit width as narrow as 5 mm
Do you have agent in US ?
No, currently we do not have any agent in US, but we are looking for representative in US area.
