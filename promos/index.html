OUR PROFILE

PT. Bina Niaga Multiusaha is a Precision Cold-Rolled Stainless Steel Mills located in prestigious Jababeka Industrial Estate Phase Two Cikarang Jawa Barat, Indonesia. Our Stainless Steel rolling mill was build on area of 15,000 m2. Since our establishment in 1996, we have contributed in developing electronic, chemical, automotive, and medical instrument industries.

We produce precision cold-rolled stainless steel with stringent accuracy in size dimension, mechanical properties and surface finish. It is highly customizable depending on each customer's requirement.

We keep growing each year and delivers more precision stainless steel products around the world for multinational customers such as Toyota, Honda, Suzuki, Samsung, Nokia, LG, Toshiba, Fujitsu and many more.
